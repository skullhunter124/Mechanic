# Mechanic - A Garage Simulator

A text-based garage management game where you diagnose car problems, order parts, and complete repairs to keep your business afloat.

## About

You run a small garage with one lift. Customers bring their cars with various problems. Your job is to:
- Diagnose what's wrong with each vehicle
- Order the right parts for the repair
- Complete repairs correctly to earn money and build reputation
- Pay your weekly rent or face bankruptcy

## How to Play

### The Basics
1. **Wait for customers** - Cars will arrive at your garage
2. **Bring them in** - Accept a job to start working on a vehicle
3. **Run diagnostics** - Use diagnostic tools to identify problems
4. **Order parts** - Select the parts you think are needed
5. **Complete the repair** - Install the parts and hope you chose correctly!

### Time Management
- Each day has three time periods: **Morning → Afternoon → Evening**
- Actions like diagnostics and repairs take time
- When it's evening, you can **end the day** to advance to the next morning
- Rent is due every **7 days**

### Money & Reputation
- Start with **€150**
- Earn money by completing repairs correctly
- Build reputation through successful jobs
- Higher reputation unlocks more customers and upgrades
- **Rent is €200 per week** - don't run out of money!

### Parts Categories
Parts are organized into categories for easier selection:
- **Basic Maintenance** - Oil, filters, belts, fluids
- **Brakes** - Pads, rotors, calipers
- **Engine** - Gaskets, sensors, cooling components
- **Electrical** - Battery, alternator, bulbs
- **Suspension & Steering** - Shocks, control arms, CV axles
- **Transmission** - Clutch, flywheel
- **Exhaust** - Muffler, catalytic converter
- **Climate Control** - A/C components
- **Wheels & Tyres** - Tyres, valves
- **Performance** - Turbo, intercooler (for turbocharged cars)

### Upgrades
Purchase upgrades to expand your capabilities:
- **Second Lift** - Work on two cars at once
- **Diagnostic Computer** - More accurate diagnostics
- **Engine Hoist** - Pull engines for major repairs
- **Welder** - Fix exhausts and fabricate parts
- **Spray Gun** - Paint work
- And more...

## Features

- **Text-based interface** with atmospheric writing
- **Customer dialogue** - Each customer has a personality
- **Diagnostic system** - Investigate problems before repairing
- **Parts ordering** - Parts take time to arrive
- **Reputation system** - Better rep = better customers
- **Random events** - Unexpected opportunities and challenges
- **Auto-save** - Progress is saved automatically
- **Win condition** - Build enough reputation to "make it"

## Tips

1. **Read the diagnostics carefully** - They reveal what's actually wrong
2. **Don't guess on repairs** - Wrong parts waste money and hurt reputation
3. **Watch your rent countdown** - Always keep enough money for rent
4. **Build reputation early** - Better customers pay more
5. **Order parts wisely** - Some parts are in stock, others take days
6. **Use the correct parts** - Using wrong parts results in unhappy customers
